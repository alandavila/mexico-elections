Geographical party preferences by state during Mexico's presidential elections

The present analysis uses presidential voting data from the Instituto Nacional Electoral. It contains the votes given to each party or coalition during the 2012 presidential election. The votes are registered for each of the "voting districts." I am interested in showing the distribution electoral preferences for each party-coallition over the country. I aggregated the votes for each state and coallition and plotted them in a of Mexico. I color coded the percentage of votes obtained in each state. Darker color means higher percentage of votes. The percentage of votes was normalized by state population in order to show a meaninful geopraphical distribution of electoral suppor and remove the bias to to state population (STEP PENDING...).
